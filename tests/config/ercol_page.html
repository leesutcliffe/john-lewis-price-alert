Product description

Product code: 80311501

Practical and stylish, this contemporary two drawer bedside table is ideal for keeping your bedroom essentials within easy reach. Beautifully crafted from solid oak and veneers, and finished in a contemporary matt finish, it features traditional dovetail and finger joints for extra strength.

Shalstone was inspired by ercol's mid-century style, and has been reinvented exclusively for John Lewis.
